Use this calculator to figure out what you will pay each month for your mortgage — the amount of money you intend to borrow to buy your new home. Enter the numbers in the box for each item, or use the slider. Your monthly payment will appear on the right.

That monthly payment includes repaying what you've borrowed (the principal) as well as the bank's fee for borrowing the money (the interest).
