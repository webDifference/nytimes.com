    The Neediest Cases Fund

    This is the 106th annual campaign of The New York Times Neediest Cases Fund, which runs from Oct. 15, 2017, to Jan. 12, 2018. The Fund has provided direct assistance to those struggling in New York and beyond.

    The New York Times Neediest Cases Fund has raised over $294 million since it was established in 1911. This campaign runs from Oct. 15, 2017, to Jan. 12, 2018.

    The New York Times Company underwrites all administrative costs incurred by the fund. The Neediest Cases Fund invests unrestricted contributions of $100,000 or more from trusts and estates in an endowment; the income will go into the next year’s campaign.

    Nicholas Kristof discusses the newest beneficiary organization, the International Rescue Committee.
